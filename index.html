About Titilope Fatima Daniel

Costume & Fashion Designer

Education

Titilope holds a Bachelor's degree in Theater Arts from the University of Ilorin, Nigeria, and a second degree from the University of Lagos. She further honed her skills with a Master's degree from the University of Minnesota, USA.

Career

Based in both Minnesota, USA and Lagos, Nigeria, Titilope has designed costumes for numerous theater productions, film projects, and fashion shows. Her work beautifully merges traditional African aesthetics with contemporary design principles.

Theater Arts & Costume Design

Theater arts encompass a wide range of disciplines related to the performance and production of theatrical works. Costume design plays a pivotal role in bringing characters to life and enhancing storytelling through visual artistry.

At TeeFabric, we specialize in creating costumes that not only look stunning but also serve the narrative purpose of each production. Our designs consider historical accuracy, character development, and practical performance needs.
